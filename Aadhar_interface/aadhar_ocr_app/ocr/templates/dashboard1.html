info About DocuLens AI

DocuLens AI is an intelligent document processing platform designed to automatically extract and digitize structured identity data from Indian Aadhar cards. By simply uploading an image, our custom-trained AI pipeline instantly isolates the ID, cleans the image, and extracts the user's Full Name, Aadhar Number, Date of Birth, and Gender without any manual data entry.
